Strengthening Prevention

Educating vulnerable communities about the risks of trafficking through awareness campaigns, workshops, and collaborations with local leaders, with a focus on addressing root causes like poverty, lack of education, and gender inequality.

Providing Comprehensive Support

Offering holistic care and rehabilitation services for survivors, including legal assistance, psychological counseling, healthcare, vocational training, and safe housing to help them reintegrate into society.

Advocating for Policy Change

Actively lobbying for stronger national and international laws to combat human trafficking, working with governments and law enforcement to ensure stricter penalties for traffickers, and promoting victim-centered policies that prioritize protection and recovery for survivors.

Cultivating Leadership

Empowering survivors of human trafficking to become advocates and leaders in their communities by providing them with leadership training, mentorship, and support to share their stories and influence policy changes.

Who We Are

Aafanta Nepal / KIN Nepal is a national non-profit, nongovernmental organization (NGO) dedicated to ending human trafficking and gender-based violence in Nepal. Since our inception in 2008, we have been providing comprehensive and holistic support to survivors while actively advocating to address the root causes of exploitation in Nepal.
